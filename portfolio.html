Finances

As a descendant of a Dutch governmental accountant, who worked for the Ministry of Finance in the Dutch colonies, I followed my grandfather's passion for financial numbers.

Working for more than 12 years as bookkeeper for a group with 2 inter-connected enterprises, as co-founder & co-owner, manager over finances and was responsible to produce the financial statements. The group had over 50 employees and almost 2M global sales per year.

Working the first years directly under supervision professional accountants, I learned all the bookkeeping procedures. After 3 years, I started to produce the statements and declaration without their supervision. Several governmental audits were issued after that, without corrective consequences for the company.

I developed programs under Excel & VBA, that automated the financial transactions and imported the registrations in the book-keeping software. These tools made us gain tremendous time and increased our work capacity.
