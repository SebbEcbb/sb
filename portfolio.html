Finances

As descendant of a Dutch gouvernmental accountant, who worked for the Financial Ministry in the Dutch colonies,
I followed my grandfather's passion for financial numbers.

Working for 12 years as bookkeeper for a group of 2 inter-connected companies, as co-owner and co-manager, I was responsible to produce the financial statements. The group had around 50 employees and avegage 2M global gross sales per year.

Working the first years directly under supervision and direction of the professional accountant, I learned all the procedures. After 3 years, I started to operate without supervision. Several gourvenmental audits were issued after that, without any corrective consequences for the company.

I developed programs under Excel & Vba, that automated the financial transactions and imported the registrations in the bookeeping software. These tools made me gain tremendous time and increasedmy work capcity.
