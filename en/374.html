    When an angel
    knocks on your door

    Improvisation #374

    When an angel
    knocks on your door

    This is not for those on their way to hell

    Hell starts when man is so dull and hardened
    That he cannot love anything
    See no beauty
    Feel no magic
    Nor thankfulness or happiness
    Not even for being alive

    He is so full of complaint
    That nothing worthy can enter
    Heart or mind

    If God himself knocked on his door
    To tell him a secret to happiness
    Or sing him a song of beauty
    With a message of love
    Or a key for life
    He would zap it

    Would we ever be so restless
    And so much without peace
    That we become unable to consider
    For more than 30 seconds
    To understand something
    Of what he was trying to tell us ?

    How in hell can a lazy & restless generation
    Hope to ever find peace & fortune ?

    With quietness of spirit
    And stillness inside
    We might find perfect peace
    For a few minutes

    Simply to let
    The music happen
    As an effortless flow

    The restless can’t

    Sébastien Badel

    alias Aman
    Belledonne, Rhône-Alpes,
    France, September 29, 2024
