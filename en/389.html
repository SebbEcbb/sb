    Improvisation #389

    A moment

    Hear the music
    Meditation

    Awareness
    Only one moment.

    Everlasting
    Eternal now
    Only one.

    Always was
    Is and will
    Past, presence, future
    All in one.

    There we touch
    Exaltation
    Joy & praise

    It is there
    In the moment.

    Stop thinking
    Rumination
    Empty talk inside.

    Quietness
    Silence
    Peace of mind.

    And we start to hear
    Not always
    If we let it happen.

    Then
    Only then
    Synchronisation

    Unique vibrations
    Every time
    Frequencies of music.

    Collection of vibrations
    Collection of ideas
    Rich and poor
    Altogether.

    We get ideas
    Rich and poor
    Altogether.

    A batch of energy
    We get energy.

    A dance of harmonies
    Progressions
    We start dancing.

    The soul starts dancing
    With harmonies & progressions.

    The soul starts dancing
    On rhythms of music.

    A language
    Transmission of knowledge
    Culture, history
    Series of experiences.

    When the heart hears
    It gets the essence of it all.

    Music has a source
    The origin
    Music has a purpose
    The destination
    It is also in between
    The way, the path.

    Like life
    When reaching destination
    The music starts again
    New & different
    Every time again

    There is only one moment.

    Hallelu Yah

    Sébastien Badel
